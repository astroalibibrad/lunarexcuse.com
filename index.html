Privacy Policy

Effective July 4, 2026

Lunar Excuse is built to be simple, and that includes how it treats your data. Here's the whole picture, plainly:

What the app stores

  • Your date of birth, stored securely on your device only (used to calculate your zodiac sign).
  • Your mood entries (date, time, and which mood you logged), stored on your device only.

None of this is uploaded to us, sold, or shared with advertisers. We don't have servers that store your personal information, because we don't need them.

What leaves your device

When the app generates a quip for you, it sends only your zodiac sign, the current moon phase, and your mood rating (Great, OK, or Bad) to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a personalized line of text. No name, no birthday, no identifying information is included in that request.

What we don't do

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising or ad networks
  • No account creation or sign-in required
  • No selling or sharing of your data with third parties
